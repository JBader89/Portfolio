Mastermind Flash Game

This was a project I created for my own pleasure and personal training while I was abroad in Hong Kong. The idea behind it was to create a Mastermind puzzle game written entirely in Flash ActionScript 3.0. To play, drag and drop different colors to try to figure out the hidden code. A red dot signifies right color, right spot. A white dot signifies right color, wrong spot. You get 10 guesses. Try to crack the code.
