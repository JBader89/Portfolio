Maya Animation

This was my final project for a week-long tutorial on Desktop Maya, a 3D animation software. During the tutorial I learned about rendering, rigging, tweening, lighting, using a camera, and how to put together a short animation. I also made all of the pieces from scratch. The project is based on a famous chess game played between two grandmasters in the 19th century.
